Dental Mechanics

(12'TH SCIENCE BIOLOGY AND MATHS)

What is B.Sc. Nursing?

Dental Mechanics is a 3-year long course.eligibility to which is BDS/MBBS degree holders from a recognized institution. The admission for this course is based on the rank obtained in NEET i.e. National Eligibility cum Entrance test. The program consists of papers and a research thesis. It encompasses the study and epidemiology of community oral health, oral health promotion, provision and funding of oral health care, and issues in the planning and management of public oral health services.

The initial salary in these positions varies from INR 4 to 20 Lacs per year.

Diploma in Dental Mechanics: What is it about?

Dental Mechanics is a graduate discipline in the Dental Surgery course and it is pertaining to the study of oral or dental health problems of a whole group or community of people living in a same geographical area. In this degree course, candidates will learn about the legal and social aspects of dentistry. They will also learn about conducting of seminars, dental camps, etc. and biostatistics. Diploma in Dental Mechanics Course includes clinical, practical and theoretical sessions and the subjects covered in this course are: Dental public health & Dental Epidemiology, Preventive Dentistry, Environmental Health, Public Health Practice & Administration Family planning, Behavioral Sciences, Public health nutrition, Public health education, Epidemiology and Biostatistics.

Diploma in Dental Mechanics: Eligibility

To pursue the Diploma in Dental Mechanics course the candidate is required to complete a BDS or MBBS degree from an Indian affiliated institution. Also they are required to get aggregate of 60% marks in qualifying examinations.

Diploma in Dental Mechanics Admission Process

All India Level Entrance Examinations are conducted for admission of students to this course and candidates will have to get through the entrance examination to seek admission to the Diploma in Dental Mechanics course in the aforesaid government Colleges.

  1. KEAM
  2. NEET
  3. PGIMER
  4. GCET
  5. JIPMER
  6. CETPPMC

Diploma in Dental Mechanics: Career Prospects

Candidates with diploma degree in Dental Mechanics can join the government organizations such as Public Health Foundation of India or other government and private-owned dental hospitals. In the case of candidates with interest towards teaching profession, they can look for jobs as lecturers in government/private dental colleges. These professionals can get job opportunities not only in India, but they can also find employment opportunities abroad since dental surgeons are always in higher demand in foreign countries like the USA and the UK. They can work in many different settings, such as schools, health care centers, rehabilitation facilities, prisons or private practice.
